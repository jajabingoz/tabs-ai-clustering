Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI tab clustering work?

TabsAI uses advanced language models to analyze your tab content and automatically group related tabs by topic, project, or theme. Our AI reads page titles, descriptions, and content to create intelligent clusters.

Is my browsing data private and secure?

Yes! We only process the content you explicitly analyze. Your browsing history, personal data, and tab content are never stored permanently. All processing happens securely and data is deleted after analysis.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll continue to have access to premium features until the end of your current billing period.

What happens if I exceed my usage limit?

On the free plan, you'll need to wait until next month or upgrade. Pro and Business plans have generous limits, and we'll notify you before you approach them. Business plan has unlimited usage.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied with TabsAI, contact our support team for a full refund within 30 days of your purchase.
